CACTI

Cacti are unique indoor plants known for their striking shapes and low maintenance needs. Adapted to store water in their thick stems, they thrive with minimal watering. Popular varieties include the Golden Barrel Cactus with its round, spiny form, and the Bunny Ear Cactus with its pad-like segments. The Christmas Cactus adds a splash of color with its vibrant holiday blooms, while the Prickly Pear Cactus features paddle-shaped pads and colorful fruit. Ideal for sunny spots, cacti bring a touch of desert beauty to your home with minimal care.
