SACCULENTS & CACTI

Succulents and cacti are popular indoor plants known for their unique shapes, low maintenance, and ability to thrive in various environments. Succulents, like Echeveria and Aloe Vera, have thick, fleshy leaves that store water, allowing them to survive with minimal watering. Cacti, such as the Saguaro and Christmas Cactus, are also drought-tolerant, characterized by their spiky exteriors and fascinating forms. These plants are perfect for sunny spots and are ideal for busy individuals or those new to gardening. Their diverse range of textures, colors, and sizes makes them versatile for different decor styles, from modern to rustic. Additionally, they are excellent for creating striking arrangements or adding a touch of greenery to small spaces like desktops or shelves.
